About Our School

Bharat Bhakt Chandrashekhar Azad Middle School, Maihar is committed to providing quality education with discipline, safety, and moral values. Our school focuses on overall development of students through smart learning, experienced teachers, and a supportive environment.


We believe every child deserves a strong foundation in academics, communication skills, and life values. Our goal is to help students become confident, responsible, and successful in life.


We offer Hindi & English Medium education with special focus on English speaking, competitive exam preparation, and practical learning.
